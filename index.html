Narrative Visualization

Summarized statistics of major immigrant visa issues in last 15 years. Attempt to visualize data obtained from USCIS in order to gain understanding of incline and decline of visa offered by USCIS to help citizen of countries across the globe. Data can become real granular, hence study has been kept to focus on major time (year) intervals to emphasize trends and and conclusive remarks.
